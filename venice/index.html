This can then be used to locate the device in event of theft, through an Android application, and even then used to disable the device through short-circuiting the battery to the solid-state drive! Alessandro Verona, Gianpietro Romanin, Paolo Turello, Stefano Driussi

MyGreens is a geolocated cashless marketplace for purchasing and discovering local produce. After purchase, users are incentivised to leave reviews, and can also subscribe to a producer for regular deliveries, or bulk discounts.

3rd place

byU

Andrea Masciadri, Antonello Fodde, Chai Botta, Jole Belgiorno

Team ByU created an app and a security locker to improve the customer experience in a store: with the app he will be able to scan the locker attached to the product using the smartphone's NFC reader and he will see the product inserted in a shopping cart with its information and price. After that he will be able to buy it using Paypal. Once the transaction has succeeded, by scanning the NFC locker again he will be able to unlock and so detach it. Now just take the product skipping the queue at the store.
